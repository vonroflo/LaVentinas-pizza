Our Story

20 Years on Bellflower Blvd

LaVentina's has been serving the City of Lakewood for over 20 years. Our homemade sauce is created using only the finest and freshest ingredients. We cut fresh vegetables daily and our dough is prepared fresh every morning.

We hand-toss every pizza, made to order. Our crust is thick and filling, our sauce is unique and deliciously different, and our product is never oily. Give us a chance to satisfy your appetite and you will not be disappointed.
